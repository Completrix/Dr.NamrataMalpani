vedic astrology

  • Sun Sign

    The sun is a natural source of energy that boosts health, uplifts mood, and enhances vitality. Let its warmth rejuvenate your mind and body.
  • moon Sign

    Your Moon Sign represents your emotional self, intuition, and inner moods. It reflects how you respond to feelings, relationships, and changes in your life.
  • Planets

    Planets in astrology symbolize different aspects of life, such as emotions, relationships, and career, influencing our personality and experiences based on their positions.
  • Ascendant

    The Ascendant (or rising sign) represents how you present yourself to the world and the first impression you make. It influences your outward personality, appearance, and approach to life
  • Twelve Houses

    The Twelve Houses in astrology represent different life areas, such as relationships, career, and personal growth, shaping how we experience and approach these aspects of life.
  • Nakshatras

    Nakshatras are the 27 lunar constellations in Vedic astrology, each influencing a person’s emotions, traits, and life path based on the Moon's position at birth.

baby names by nakshatra

Choosing a baby name by Nakshatra involves selecting a name based on the lunar constellation the baby is born under. Each Nakshatra has specific syllables and meanings, guiding the selection of names that align with the baby’s celestial influence.

  • career

    Career in astrology is guided by the 10th House and the positions of planets like Saturn, Jupiter, and Mercury, providing insights into professional strengths, ideal careers, and growth opportunities.

  • relationship
    relationships are influenced by the positions of Venus, Mars, and the 7th House, which governs partnerships. Analyzing your natal chart can reveal compatibility, emotional needs, and ways to improve communication and connection in relationships.

  • finance

    Finance in astrology is influenced by the 2nd House of wealth, the 8th House of shared resources, and planets like Venus and Jupiter, offering insights into financial habits and opportunities for prosperity.
  • business

    Business success in astrology is guided by the 10th House, 2nd House, and the positions of planets like Mercury, Venus, and Mars, revealing insights into entrepreneurial strengths and financial opportunities.
  • education

    Education in astrology is influenced by the 3rd and 5th Houses, as well as the placement of Mercury and Jupiter, offering insights into learning styles, academic strengths, and areas for growth.
  • health

    Health in astrology is influenced by the 6th House and planets like Mars and Saturn, revealing insights into physical well-being, potential health challenges, and ways to maintain balance.
